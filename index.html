CLASTIC MUSIC

Clastic music

Akin to how machine creativity uses pre-existing data to generate new forms, in Clastic Music I use rhythmic patterns in simple and compound meter as the fragmentary entities to create new music.

Clastic Music is a full-length music album also published as an interactive web-based application.

In this web application you will have the chance to play with a video synthesizer by dragging the mouse.

The nine clastic songs have different patches that you can explore.

Shortcuts:

Enter to go into full-screen mode

Esc to leave full-screen mode

M for muting and unmuting the audio
